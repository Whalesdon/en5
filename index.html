Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs)

What are NFTs?

NFTs are unique digital assets created on the blockchain. They can be everything from gaming items and digital art, to sports collectibles and real-world assets.

Ownership

NFTs are truly yours to own—no one can take them from you.

Utility

NFTs can be used in apps, games, websites, and even in real life.

Real-World Value

NFTs created with Enjin are backed by ENJ, a cryptocurrency.

Trading Fees

Get a cut whenever someone sells an NFT you created.

Rarity

Every NFT is unique, that's what non-fungible means!

Blockchain

NFTs exist on the blockchain, a decentralized ledger.
